Andre Raclette Party

Join us for a night of melted cheese and good company.

The city bustles with energy and light,

But in this cozy apartment, all is right.

Friends gather round the table with glee,

For a raclette party in New York City.

The cheese is melted and bubbling hot,

Paired with potatoes, it's the perfect pot.

We add some charcuterie and a glass of wine,

As laughter and conversation intertwine.

The streets outside may be cold and gray,

But in this warm room, it's a happy day.

We take a break from the hustle and bustle,

To enjoy this Swiss tradition, oh so rustic.

As the night wears on and the cheese is gone,

We raise our glasses, and sing a song.

To friendship, to good food, and to this city,

We'll always remember this raclette party.
